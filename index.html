  • ES6+ and BABEL

JS+Me=?

At first I did not like JS. For me it was horrible thing, beacause JS was unpredicteble especially after Java or C languages. After a while I get a job as Full Stack developer. We had much more JS in work than I expected. After month of pain I decided to do something with it, I should understand how JS works. So after work I star reading books. It was very boring, few times I even fell asleep ('You don't know JS' and 'JS:The Good Parts' - was very helpfull). I needed another way. So I just playing with JS. Made small projects which covers those areas which I badly understand. Soon I loved JS. I understand how flexible it is, how cool it is (Especially with ES6+).

ES6+ and BABEL

'Function declaration', 'hoisting', 'right side assignment' - those things make new developers cry and make developers with few years of experience to feel pride, because they already understand that complicated staff. ES6+ syntax make life much more simplier (life of Web Developer of course, I don't think that Web desiners feel better beacuse of ES6+). And I just like new syntax, it's much more easier to understand what another developer wants to do if he used class declaration from ES6+ rather than 'prototypes'. And if it allows me to develop faster and more understandable, why I should not use it? For browsers which still doesn`t have ES6 support I use babel compiler

GSAP

Do you see those dope animations in top-design websites??? GSAP is probably the best tool for creating and managing animations. With this library your site become more smooth and alive.
